4. Research meaning:
   If ATR, volume, RSI, MACD, and volatility behave differently in different regimes, then one fixed feature-selection model is too rigid.
   This supports the next idea: Regime-Adaptive Bayesian LASSO, where each market regime can have its own important indicators.
